Text Editor Documentation

Every now and then, in the SalemModLoader discord server, people will be like:

Townie123:

Does anybody know how to use the TextEditor plugin?

or

Townie123:

How can I change text in ToS2?

So, here is how you do it:

1. Dump the XML of the game through the Mod Actions menu. \n2. Open the dumped XML of your locale (i.e. StringTable.xx-XX.xml). \n3. Use Control + F to find the line you are looking for. You may also go into the settings of the plugin and enable "Show Keys" \n4. Copy the ENTIRE line, including the and tags \n5. In the "Custom" folder (in the same directory the "Dumped" folder is in), create a new file with the same name as the dumped file (i.e. StringTable.XX-XX.xml) \n6. Paste in your entry and change your text. \n7. Surround it with on the line above and on the line below.

Here is an example file:

StringTable.en-US.xml

\n \n %role%! ]]> \n \n \n \nThis dude is up to some shit... \nRomeo! Romeo! Wherefore art thou Romeo? \nYou look at the moon, in it's beauty, it's fullness... just kidding! you're probably dead if there's a [[#51]] or a level one [[#42]]. \ngg ez scrub \n

Some things to note:

1. Do not change anything surrounded in <> or "CDATA" \n2. Anything in double square brackets is a reference [[:X]] is a keyword, and [[#X]] is a role. Because of this, no keywords or rols can be in your search with Control + F.

And with that, happy searching!
